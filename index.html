Ahsoka Tano

About

Ahsoka Tano, a Togruta female, was the Padawan learner to Anakin Skywalker and a hero of the Clone Wars. Alongside Anakin, she grew from headstrong student into a mature leader. But her destiny laid along a different path than the Jedi. After a series of events, General Tano left the Jedi Order to find her own path. This path leads her to help form the rebel alliance against the Empire. She is well known for wielding two lighsabers in a reverse grip; an unusual style of using a lightsaber but she made it her greatest strength, and being adept at using the force.
